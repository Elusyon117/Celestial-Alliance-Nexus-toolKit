# Third-party Sources and Packaged Artwork

Celestial Nexus is a fan-made tool that references Star Citizen names, public information, community databases, and external media. All trademarks, data, names, and artwork remain the property of their respective owners.

## Source categories used by the application

Depending on availability, modules may reference:

- Roberts Space Industries and the official Star Citizen status service.
- Star Citizen Wiki / starcitizen.tools.
- UEX Corp and community market data.
- Erkul and other ship/component databases.
- SC Trade Tools and hauling resources.
- Regolith and community mining resources.
- Wikelo community trade/project references.
- Dymerz and MrKraken localization work.
- Clearly labeled community news, rumor, leak, and datamine feeds.

External availability, accuracy, licensing, and data formats may change independently of this repository.

## Packaged Wikelo artwork

The WEBP references in `assets/wikelo/` were included with the supplied toolkit package to satisfy the local paths used by the application. `monde-crimson-reference.svg` is a locally created compatibility illustration included with the previous package.

Before public redistribution beyond the owner's intended community use, review each asset's provenance and permission status. Replace any asset that cannot be redistributed with owner-created or properly licensed artwork.
